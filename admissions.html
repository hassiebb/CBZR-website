Start Your Refereeing Journey Today

Join our basketball refereeing programs to develop your skills, gain certification, and uphold the values of fair play and integrity in the game.

How to Enroll

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form to express your interest in joining our refereeing programs.

2

Provide Documentation

Submit any required documents, such as proof of prior experience or identification.

3

Attend Orientation

Participate in an orientation session to learn about our programs and expectations.

4

Begin Training

Start your journey with our comprehensive training sessions designed to prepare you for officiating basketball games.

Enrollment Requirements

Basic Knowledge of Basketball

Applicants should have a fundamental understanding of basketball rules and gameplay.

Recommendation Letter

A letter from a coach or mentor supporting your application is encouraged but not mandatory.

Commitment to Fair Play

Applicants must demonstrate a commitment to upholding the values of sportsmanship and integrity.

Physical Fitness

Referees should be in good physical condition to effectively officiate games.
