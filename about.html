Our Story

Shaping Referees, Elevating the Game

BREF was founded with the mission to empower basketball referees with the knowledge, skills, and integrity needed to officiate games at the highest standards. Our journey has been one of dedication to fair play and professionalism.

2000

Established to provide structured training programs for aspiring basketball referees, promoting fairness and respect in the game.

2008

Launched advanced certification courses to ensure referees are equipped to handle high-stakes games with confidence.

2015

Expanded our reach globally, collaborating with international organizations to standardize refereeing practices.

2022

Introduced mentorship programs to connect experienced referees with newcomers, fostering a supportive community.

Our Mission

To educate and empower basketball referees to uphold the principles of integrity, fairness, and professionalism in every game they officiate.

Our Vision

To be the leading organization in basketball referee education, setting the global standard for excellence in officiating.

Core Values

Integrity

We instill the importance of honesty and fairness in referees, ensuring every game is officiated with impartiality.

Collaboration

We build a strong community of referees, coaches, and players to enhance teamwork and mutual respect in basketball.

Excellence

We provide top-tier education and training to empower referees to perform at their best in every game.

Global Standards

We aim to unify refereeing practices worldwide, promoting consistency and professionalism in basketball officiating.
