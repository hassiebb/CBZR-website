Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information as part of our mission to educate and certify basketball referees with professionalism and integrity.

1. Introduction

When you engage with Bref, you're trusting us with your information. We value this trust and are committed to safeguarding your data while empowering you to take control of your information.

This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can manage or delete your information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to referees and basketball enthusiasts. The types of information we collect include:

2.1 Information You Provide

When you register for our programs or use our services, you may provide us with personal information such as:

  • Your name and contact details
  • Account credentials
  • Payment information for course fees
  • Preferences for communication and updates

2.2 Automatic Information

We automatically collect certain information when you interact with our website or services:

  • Device and browser information
  • Usage statistics and log data
  • Location data if enabled
  • Technical details about your interactions

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to support our mission of educating and certifying basketball referees. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize our training programs
  • Process registrations and payments
  • Send updates about courses, events, and resources
  • Ensure security and verify participant identities
  • Improve our educational offerings and services

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

4.1 With Your Consent

We will share your information with third parties when you explicitly authorize us to do so.

4.2 For Legal Reasons

We may share your information if required to comply with legal obligations or to protect the rights and safety of Bref and its community:

  • To comply with applicable laws or regulations
  • To enforce our Terms of Service
  • To address fraud, security, or technical issues
  • To protect the rights and safety of Bref and its users

5. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your information and implement measures to protect it from unauthorized access or misuse:

  • Using SSL encryption for secure communication
  • Regularly reviewing our data handling practices
  • Restricting access to personal information to authorized personnel

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access and review your information
  • The right to correct inaccuracies
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to limit or object to data processing

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with the effective date.

Your continued use of our services after updates signifies your acceptance of the revised policy.
